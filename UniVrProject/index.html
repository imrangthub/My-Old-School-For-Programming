Bangladeshi government has passed an ordinance making national ID cards mandatory for citizens. ID cards has, however, be issued only for voting purposes in the beginning to encourage eligible people to register their names on the electoral roll at the registration centers. Now this ID card is mandatory for all kind of verifications such as Bank loan or Account, Buying assets etc. in near future it will be an essential element for any valid transition.

Rerequisites to register for National ID card:

Applicants must be adult (18 years) ?
Citizen of Bangladesh ?
Applicants should apply in prescribed Application form ?
Submit application to Election commission office or concern District/ Thana Election Commission office ?
Application addressing the Project Director, PERP, Election Commission Office

What will I do if I lost my National ID card (NID)?
Make a General Dairy in the local police station including the NID number ?
Collect the free application form from Election Commission Office ?
Fill-up the form with Name, Address, issued NID or Voter ID number and signature along with the GD copy. ?
The Voter number can be collected from the local Election commission office ?
Submit the filled application to Election commission office or concern District/ Thana Election Commission office by addressing the Project Director, PERP, Election Commission Office ?
After submitting the form, a received memo will be provided with a delivery date ?
The full process is free of cost and will take 39 days from application submission date.
